Frequently Asked Questions

How long is the average wait time?

Wait times vary based on party size, day of the week, and time of day. Current estimated wait times are displayed above and are updated in real-time.

Can I specify seating preferences?

Yes, you can mention your seating preferences in the "Special Requests" field, and we'll do our best to accommodate them based on availability.

How will I be notified when a table is ready?

You'll receive a text message and/or email notification when your table is ready or about to become available.

What if I can't respond right away?

We hold your spot for 15 minutes after notification. If you need more time, please contact us directly at (555) 123-4567.

Can I join the waiting list for a future date?

Yes, you can join our waiting list up to 7 days in advance. We recommend trying our reservation system first for future dates.

Is there a fee to join the waiting list?

No, joining our waiting list is completely free of charge.

Prefer a Guaranteed Reservation?

If you have a specific date and time in mind, we recommend making a reservation in advance.
