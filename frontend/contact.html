Frequently Asked Questions

Do you offer private dining?

Yes, we offer private dining options for special occasions. Please contact us for more information about our private dining rooms and catering services.

Can I make changes to my reservation?

Yes, you can modify your reservation up to 24 hours before your scheduled time. Please call us or use our online booking system.

Do you cater to dietary restrictions?

Absolutely! We can accommodate various dietary needs including vegetarian, vegan, gluten-free, and allergen-specific requests. Please inform us when making your reservation.

Is there a dress code?

We maintain a smart casual dress code. While formal attire is not required, we ask that guests refrain from wearing athletic wear, beachwear, or overly casual clothing.
